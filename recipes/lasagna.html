Mitch's special lasagna

Mitch's special lasagna has been handcrafted and perfected over generations of his family, finally ending with the last of kin, never to be handed down again.

    Ingredients

  • Tomato+Meat Sauce
  • Pasta
  • Mozzarella (2cup)
  • Cottage cheese mixture (3cup)
  • Mitch's secret sauce

    Instructions

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Layer pasta, cheeses, and sauce in 3 layers
  3. Milk the creator
  4. Go to hospital
  5. Add the secret sauce to top, place in pan
  6. Cook 45 minutes
